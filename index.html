How to Delete Your Account

If you no longer wish to use the One Library Manager App and want your account and data removed, follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account

  • Open the app and log in to your account.
  • Go to the Logout screen.
  • Tap the “Delete My Account” button.
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently removed from our Firebase Auth system.
  • All associated data such as your name, phone number, room allotment, and fee records will be deleted.

Data Retention

No personal data is retained. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
